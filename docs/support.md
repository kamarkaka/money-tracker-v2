## FAQ

### How do I back up my data?
Go to **Settings > Export Data**. This saves all your data as a JSON file that you can store anywhere.

### How do I transfer data to a new device?
Export your data on the old device, transfer the JSON file (via AirDrop, email, etc.), then open the app on the new device and go to **Settings > Import Data**.

### I accidentally deleted a transaction. Can I undo it?
Deleted transactions cannot be recovered unless you have a previously exported backup. We recommend exporting your data regularly.

### Does the app sync across devices?
No. All data is stored locally on your device. Use the export/import feature to move data between devices.

### How do I delete all my data?
Delete the app from your device. All data is stored locally and will be permanently removed.
