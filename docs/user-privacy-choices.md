# Your Privacy Choices

**Money Tracker 2**
Last updated: March 23, 2026

## Your Data, Your Control

Money Tracker 2 is designed with a local-first approach. All your financial data stays on your device. You have full control over your data at all times.

## What Data Is Stored

The app stores the following data locally on your device:
- Transactions (description, amount, date, category)
- Accounts and institutions
- Categories and budgets
- Tags and category rules
- App settings (theme, language)

**None of this data is sent to any server.**

## Your Choices

### Export Your Data
You can export all your data at any time as a JSON file through **Settings > Export Data**. This creates a complete backup that you own and control.

### Import Data
You can import previously exported data through **Settings > Import Data**. This replaces existing data on the device with the imported file.

### Delete Your Data
To delete all your data, you can:
- **Delete the app** — Removing Money Tracker 2 from your device permanently deletes all stored data.
- **Import an empty file** — Overwrites existing data.

Since no data is stored on external servers, deleting the app is a complete and permanent deletion. There is nothing to request removal of.

### Opt-Out of Data Collection
There is nothing to opt out of. Money Tracker 2 does not collect, transmit, or share any data.

## Apple App Store

If you purchase a subscription through the App Store, Apple processes your payment. You can manage your subscription and review Apple's data practices in your device's **Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions**.
