# Privacy Policy

**Money Tracker 2**
Last updated: March 23, 2026

## Overview

Money Tracker 2 is a personal finance tracking app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.** Money Tracker 2 stores all data locally on your device. No information is transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Specifically, we do **not** collect:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Financial data (transactions, account balances, budgets)
- Usage analytics or behavioral data
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Cookies or tracking technologies

## Data Storage

All financial data you enter — including transactions, categories, accounts, budgets, tags, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using a local database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

## Data Export and Import

The app provides an export feature that allows you to save your data as a JSON file. This file is created locally on your device and shared only through the method you choose (e.g., AirDrop, email, Files app). We have no access to exported files.

## Third-Party Services

Money Tracker 2 does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## In-App Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Apple's privacy policy governs how payment information is handled. We do not receive or store your payment details.

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since the app collects no data from any user, it is safe for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
